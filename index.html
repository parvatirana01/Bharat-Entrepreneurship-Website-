About The Event

The New Era of Entrepreneurship

Join us for the Bharat Entrepreneurship Conclave (BEC) at Chitkara University, Punjab! Our one-day event brings together entrepreneurs from across the country to learn, connect, and share experiences.With 11 expert speakers covering topics from ideation to scaling, attendees will have the opportunity to gain valuable insights, network, and explore new business opportunities.
